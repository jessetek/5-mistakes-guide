What Makes La Mirada
Worth Calling Home.

Genuinely Quiet Neighborhoods

La Mirada was master-planned in the 50s with curving residential streets and minimal through-traffic. Listen for yourself — it's noticeable.

🎭

La Mirada Theatre for the Performing Arts

Regional theater that punches well above its city size. Live shows, kids programs, community events — adds real lifestyle value.

🏊

Outstanding Recreation Programs

Splash! La Mirada Aquatics Center, Behringer Park, La Mirada Regional Park. The city actually invests in its parks and rec.

🏠

Stable Long-Term Values

La Mirada home values track upward steadily — no boom-bust like flashier cities. Good for long-term holders.
