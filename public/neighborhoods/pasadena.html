Why Pasadena

What Makes Pasadena
Worth Calling Home.

🏛️

Walkable Old Town + Real Downtown

Few LA cities have this kind of historic, walkable downtown — restaurants, theaters, galleries, the Pasadena Playhouse, and a true urban experience.

🎓

Pasadena Unified + Private School Density

PUSD has strong feeders, and Pasadena has one of LA's highest concentrations of top-tier private schools (Polytechnic, Mayfield, Westridge).

🌹

Cultural Capital

Norton Simon Museum, the Huntington, the Rose Bowl, Caltech adjacent. The cultural depth of a much bigger city in a residential package.

🏠

Architectural Crown Jewel of LA

More original Craftsman homes than any other US city. Spanish Revival, Tudor, mid-century modern, and contemporary all coexist here.
