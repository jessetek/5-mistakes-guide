Why Long Beach

What Makes Long Beach
Worth Calling Home.

🌊

Coastal Lifestyle

Beach access from $600K condos to $3M+ Naples Island homes. The ocean is part of everyday life here.

🏘️

Distinct Neighborhoods

Each Long Beach neighborhood has its own personality — Bixby Knolls is family-suburban, Belmont Shore is walkable-cool, Downtown is urban.

🍳

Brunch & Coffee Culture

From Steamed in Belmont Shore to Berlin Bistro in the East Village, Long Beach has one of the best food scenes in SoCal.

🎨

Arts & Music

The Pike Outlets, the Aquarium, the Convention Center, and a thriving live music scene make Long Beach LA's most cultural beach city.

Things to Do
