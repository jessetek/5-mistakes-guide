Why Cerritos

What Makes Cerritos
Worth Calling Home.

🎓

Top-Tier ABC School District

ABC Unified ranks in the top 5 districts statewide. Whitney High consistently lands on national top-100 lists. School quality directly drives Cerritos home values.

🏘️

Planned Community Layout

Wide streets, master-planned tracts, mature landscaping. Most homes are 1970s-era single-family with big lots — a different feel than the LA grid.

🛡️

Among the Safest in LA County

Consistently one of the lowest crime rates in the county. Families relocating from elsewhere in LA notice the difference immediately.

🍜

World-Class Food Scene

Korean BBQ at Genwa, regional Indian at Ashoka, Filipino at Goldilocks, Vietnamese at Brodard. Cerritos eats like a city ten times its size.

A real-talk breakdown of the Cerritos sub-areas — from premium zones to value plays.

Cerritos Auto Square Area

North-side homes near the auto mall — easy 605 access, mid-century single-family stock.

📍

South Cerritos / Whitney Zone

Premium pricing for homes feeding into Whitney High School. Limited inventory, multiple offers common.

📍

Cerritos Towne Center Area

Walkable to shopping, library, and performing arts center. Tight market for condos and townhomes.

📍

Cerritos Park East

Family-favorite area with tree-lined streets and proximity to Cerritos Park East and the senior center.
