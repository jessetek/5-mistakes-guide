Why Downey

What Makes Downey
Worth Calling Home.

🏫

Strong School District

Downey Unified is a cornerstone of Southeast LA. Williams Elementary earns a 9/10 GreatSchools rating, and Warren and Downey High both post 97%+ graduation rates.

🏘️

Established Neighborhoods

From Old Downey to Northwest, the housing stock is mostly single-family homes from the '50s and '60s — solid construction, big lots, character.

🚗

Easy Commute

Direct freeway access via the 5, 605, and 105. Downtown LA is 20 minutes. LAX is 30. The OC line is 15.

🌮

Real Food Scene

Porto's. Mariscos Choix. The LKSD. Buena Mesa. Legit bakeries on every corner. Downey eats better than most of LA.
