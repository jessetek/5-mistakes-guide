Why Whittier

What Makes Whittier
Worth Calling Home.

🏛️

Historic Charm

Uptown Whittier's tree-lined streets and craftsman homes feel like a different era. Greenleaf Avenue is one of LA's best walkable downtowns.

🎓

College Town Energy

Whittier College and Rio Hondo bring a steady mix of professors, students, and young professionals to the city.

🏞️

Hills & Trails

Friendly Hills, Murphy Ranch, and the Whittier Greenway Trail give you nature minutes from your front door.

🍷

Friday Night Scene

Whittier's downtown is alive on weekends — wine bars, breweries, and the Friday Night Concert series.
