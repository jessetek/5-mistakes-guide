Why Pico Rivera

What Makes Pico Rivera
Worth Calling Home.

🏡

Affordable Family Homes

Pico Rivera offers some of the best price-per-square-foot in Southeast LA. Most homes are 1,400-2,000 sqft on 5,000-6,000 sqft lots.

🌳

Family-First Vibe

Smith Park, Rio Hondo Trail, the El Rancho schools — Pico Rivera is built for families who want a backyard and a community.

🌮

Cultura Rica

From the El Rancho swap meet to the Sunday flea market, Pico Rivera's Latino roots run deep — and so does the food.

🇲🇽

Bilingual Service

Soy bilingüe. Te ayudo en inglés o español, lo que sea más cómodo para tu familia.

Things to Do

Living in Pico Rivera
Isn't Boring.

Smith Park

16-acre park with an Olympic-size pool, skate park and community events.

Pico Rivera Sports Arena

Outdoor concert and rodeo venue hosting regional Mexican music stars.

Pico Rivera Golf Club

Paved riverside trail running through Pico Rivera to the coast.
