Why Santa Monica

What Makes Santa Monica
Worth Calling Home.

🏖️

Beach + Walkability

Beach Bike Path, Third Street Promenade, Main Street, Montana Ave — walkable retail in multiple neighborhoods. Most pedestrian-friendly LA city.

💼

Silicon Beach Hub

Snap, Hulu, Riot, and 100+ tech companies based in SM. Tech-money buyer pool keeps the market hot.

🏫

SMMUSD Schools

Santa Monica-Malibu USD has well-rated elementaries. Samohi (SM High) is solid for public LA. Buyers compare to Brentwood + Beverly Hills.

💄

North of Montana

One of LA's most expensive single neighborhoods. Lots of $5M-$15M trades. Drives the Santa Monica luxury comp.
