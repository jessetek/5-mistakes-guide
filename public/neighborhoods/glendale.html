Why Glendale

What Makes Glendale
Worth Calling Home.

⛰️

Verdugo Hills Hillside Living

North Glendale rises into the Verdugos — homes with hillside character, views, and large lot sizes uncommon in LA.

🇦🇲

Strong Armenian-American Community

One of the largest Armenian-American populations in the US. Restaurants, businesses, churches, and a deep cultural identity.

🛍️

Americana at Brand + Glendale Galleria

Two major retail/dining anchors. Walkable, modern, well-connected — a real downtown experience few LA suburbs offer.

📍

Central LA Location

Direct 134, 5, 2 freeway access. Downtown LA in 15 min, Pasadena in 10, Burbank studios in 5.
