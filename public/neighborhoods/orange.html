Why Orange

What Makes Orange
Worth Calling Home.

🏛️

Old Towne Orange Historic Plaza

National Register Historic District — the most authentic walkable downtown in OC. Independent restaurants, antique row, the Plaza fountain.

🎓

Chapman University + Strong K-12

Chapman drives a steady educated population. Orange Unified has solid feeders, especially East Orange schools.

🏘️

Architectural Variety

Old Towne has 1880s Victorians and Craftsman bungalows. East Orange has 1960s ranch homes. North Orange has new construction. Real range.

🛣️

Multi-Freeway Access

5, 22, 55, 57, 91 all serve Orange. Most freeway-flexible city in central OC.
