Garden Grove Real Estate,
Cultural Heart of OC.

Garden Grove is OC's third-largest city and home to Little Saigon — the largest Vietnamese-American community in the US. Walkable Bolsa Avenue corridor, mid-priced family homes, and central OC location make it a real value play in the OC market.

Why Garden Grove

What Makes Garden Grove
Worth Calling Home.

🍜

Little Saigon Culinary Capital

The Bolsa Avenue corridor is a regional food destination — Vietnamese, Chinese, Korean, and Thai food at the highest concentration in SoCal.

📍

Central OC Location

22, 5, 405, 57, 22 freeways all serve. Disneyland 10 min, beaches 20 min, downtown LA 35 min.

🏘️

Mid-Priced OC Family Market

Median $885K — accessible OC pricing in a city with real amenities. First-time and step-up buyers both find homes here.

🌳

Real Park System

Garden Grove Park, Eastgate Park, and the Civic Center area give the city more green space than most OC cities of comparable size.
