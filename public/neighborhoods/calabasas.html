Hidden Hills' Slightly More Public Cousin.

Why Calabasas

What Makes Calabasas
Worth Calling Home.

🔐

Premium Gated Security

Hidden Hills, The Oaks, The Estates of The Oaks, Mountain View Estates — multiple gated communities with full-time security. Rare LA combination.

🏫

LVUSD Top Schools

Las Virgenes USD includes Calabasas High (top 5 in LA County) and feeders. School performance drives home values aggressively here.

⛰️

Hilltop Privacy

Most homes have hill privacy, larger lots than equivalent BH/Bel Air, and ocean breeze. Real estate that feels removed from the city.

💼

Studio + Tech Buyers

Lots of music industry, athlete, and exec residents. Stable buyer pool that doesn't flinch at $2M-$5M+ moves.

in Calabasas.

A real-talk breakdown of the Calabasas sub-areas — from premium zones to value plays.

📍

Hidden Hills (technically not Calabasas but adjacent)

Equestrian gated community. Privacy + acreage + celebrity neighbors. $5M-$30M typical.

📍

The Oaks of Calabasas

Premier gated community. Custom estates, top schools, $3M-$10M+ range.

📍

Calabasas Highlands

Hilltop community west of central Calabasas. Larger lots, ocean views from upper streets.

📍

Calabasas Park / Old Topanga

Slightly more attainable Calabasas. Mid-luxury entry, walkable to The Commons retail.
