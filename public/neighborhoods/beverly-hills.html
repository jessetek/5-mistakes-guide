The 90210 Standard.

Beverly Hills is what the rest of the world thinks LA is — manicured streets, top-tier schools, world-class shopping on Rodeo, and homes ranging from $3M flats to $100M+ estates in the Flats and Beverly Park. The 90210 zip alone moves homes that don't go to MLS half the time.

Why Beverly Hills

What Makes Beverly Hills
Worth Calling Home.

💄

The 90210 Brand

Few zip codes carry global recognition like 90210. Real estate values are partly the property and partly the address — and that premium is durable.

🏫

Beverly Hills USD

BHUSD is one of the highest-rated public districts in LA. BH High consistently ranks in CA's top 30. School zones drive home prices in the 8.5-acre BH district.

🏛️

Walkable Civic Core

Rodeo, BH City Hall, the Hilton, the Library, the Park — civic + retail + dining in a tight walkable square mile. Rare in LA.

💰

Off-Market Liquidity

BH luxury market trades 30%+ off-market. Pocket listings, private exposure, broker networks — knowing the right realtor matters more here than almost anywhere.

Where to Live
in Beverly Hills.

A real-talk breakdown of the Beverly Hills sub-areas — from premium zones to value plays.

📍

Beverly Hills Flats

Walkable, tree-lined streets between Wilshire and Sunset. The historic 90210 core. $5M-$30M typical.

📍

North of Sunset / Trousdale Estates

Hilltop estates, custom modern + post-and-beam. Premium of premium. $10M-$100M+.

📍

Beverly Hills Post Office (BHPO)

Hillside zip overlap with BH proper. Gated communities like Beverly Park. $5M-$200M ceiling.

📍

South of Wilshire

More attainable Beverly Hills. Smaller lots, condos, mid-luxury entry to 90210.
