Is this actually free?

Yes — 100% free. Standard message and data rates from your carrier may apply, but I never charge a dime for the alerts.

Once a week, every Thursday at 11:30am Pacific. That's it. No spam, no daily blasts, no random follow-ups.

Anytime. Just reply STOP to any text and you're out — no questions, no awkward "wait, why?" follow-ups.

Not at all. Most of the people on this list are 3–12 months away from buying or selling. The whole point is to get smart about the market before you're ready to move.
