Common Questions

Things Buyers & Sellers
Always Ask Me.

How much does it cost to work with you?

For buyers: nothing. The seller pays my commission as part of the deal. For sellers: my commission is negotiable based on the home value and the marketing plan we agree on. We talk it through before any contract gets signed.

What if I'm not ready to buy or sell yet?

Then you're exactly who I want to talk to. The best clients are the ones who plan ahead. Book a free call and I'll help you figure out your real timeline — no pressure to do anything before you're ready.

Do you only work in Downey?

Downey is home base, but I cover all of Southeast LA and most of north Orange County. Pico Rivera, Whittier, La Habra, Long Beach, Anaheim, Santa Ana, Newport Beach, Huntington Beach — I work all of it.

How fast do you respond?

Within minutes during normal hours, within an hour at night and on weekends. If you text me about a listing on a Sunday morning, I'll text you back before you finish your coffee.

¿Hablas español?

Sí, hablo español perfectamente. Te puedo ayudar en inglés o español, lo que sea más cómodo para ti y tu familia. La mayoría de mis clientes son bilingües y a veces saltamos entre los dos idiomas en una sola conversación. No hay problema.

What makes you different from other agents?

I'm local. I'm bilingual. I respond fast. I don't have a team that hands you off to someone else. You text me, you get me. And I'll tell you what's actually true about the market — even when it's not what you want to hear.
