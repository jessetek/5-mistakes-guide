About Jesse

I Am Not Your Typical Agent

I got into real estate because I saw too many people, especially millennials like me, getting taken advantage of during the biggest purchase of their lives.

I specialize in helping first time homebuyers and sellers in Downey and the surrounding cities in LA and Orange County. My approach is simple: be honest, educate my clients, and fight for the best deal possible.

When I am not closing deals, you can find me creating educational content on Instagram where I break down the home buying process in a way that actually makes sense.
