Garden Grove's median is $895K, with sub-area variation across Little Saigon adjacent, West Garden Grove. Garden Grove USD. The right neighborhood depends on your budget, your commute, and what you actually want — not what Zillow shows you first.

Pre-approval letter dictates everything downstream. I introduce you to bilingual lenders who specialize in first-time buyers. CalHFA, MyHome, FHA, conventional — they all apply differently.

Mello-Roos, supplemental tax, HOA, transfer fees. Most agents skip these. I show you the full monthly cost — including the $3-8K/yr extras — before you write the offer.
