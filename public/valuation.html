Common Questions

What Sellers
Always Ask.

Is the valuation really free?

Yes, 100% free. No credit card, no hidden fees, no "sign up to see your number" trick. I pull the comps, write up the CMA, and send it to you. Whether you ever list with me or not is your call.

Do I have to list with you to get the report?

Nope. Not even a little. My hope is that you'll love the report enough to list with me eventually — but if you just want to know your number, that's fine too. I'd rather earn your trust first.

How fast will I hear back?

Within 1 hour during business hours for the initial response (just to let you know I got it), and within 24 hours for the full CMA report. Night and weekend submissions still get next-morning turnaround.

How accurate is your valuation vs Zillow?

Zillow's Zestimate has an average 7% error. Mine is typically within 2–3% of actual sale price because I walk the comps, see the condition, and know the micro-trends in your specific block. I'm not guessing — I'm pricing like I'm the one selling.

What if my home has unique features (pool, ADU, view)?

That's exactly why you need a real CMA and not a Zestimate. Pools, ADUs, views, and upgrades can swing your home's value by $50K–$200K. I find the right comps and adjust for every one of them.

Do you cover cities outside Downey?

Yes — Pico Rivera, Whittier, La Habra, Norwalk, Long Beach, Lakewood, Anaheim, Santa Ana, Newport Beach, Huntington Beach, and most of SoCal. If your home is in LA or OC, I can probably value it.
