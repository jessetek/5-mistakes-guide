Supplemental Tax Bills

3–6 months after you close, a surprise tax bill can hit for thousands. It's called a "supplemental tax" — it makes up the difference between what the last owner paid and what your home is now worth. Most buyers have no idea this is coming.

02

Mello-Roos: The Hidden Tax

On top of your normal property taxes, many areas in LA County have Mello-Roos — an extra tax that can add $3,000–$5,000+ per year to your bill. And it won't show up in most mortgage calculators.

03

The Dual Agency Trap

In California, one agent can legally work for both you and the seller. When that happens, your agent can't tell you the seller would take a lower price — even if they know it. They get paid double, you lose out.
