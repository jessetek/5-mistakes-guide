## The honest goal (adopt this)
> **Own the Downey/Montebello map pack + the bilingual & long-tail organic lanes + branded search —
> measured by impressions, GBP calls, and qualified leads. NOT organic #1 for the bare head term.**

Organic #1 for "real estate agent" is structurally unwinnable for a solo site: the SERP is ~60-80%
consumed by an AI Overview → the Local 3-pack → directory results (Zillow/Realtor.com/Redfin/Homes.com)
whose authority you can't out-muscle. **GSC proves rank isn't the problem** (already ~3.9 for the head
term, ~2.5 branded) — the ceiling is **impressions (169/28d) = authority + GBP**, not page tweaks.
~**70% of the winnable upside is off-page work only Jesse can do** (GBP, reviews, local links).

## The 5 winnable surfaces (priority order)
1. **Local 3-pack** for "real estate agent Downey" / "realtor near me" — highest value + most winnable;
   3 slots out-CTR organic #1 and take ~44% of local clicks. Competition = ~10-30 Downey agents, not Zillow.
2. **Long-tail/hyperlocal organic** the portals under-serve: "bilingual real estate agent Downey",
   "first time home buyer Downey", neighborhood guides, "Downey housing market 2026".
3. **The Spanish-language lane** — biggest white space. "agente de bienes raíces Downey",
   "casas en venta Montebello", "realtor que habla español cerca de mí". ~13% of agents are bilingual;
   the area is heavily Hispanic; portals barely compete. Clearest win on the board.
4. **Branded search** ("Jesse Onate") — fed by Instagram; already ~2.5; grow the volume.
5. **AI Overview citations** — new 2025-26 surface; fed by GBP + reviews + schema + front-loaded copy.

## PRIORITY 0 — JESSE, THIS WEEK (the gate)
**Optimize the (already-claimed) Google Business Profile to 100%.** Map pack is GBP-fed (~32% of pack weight);
primary category is the #1 relevance lever.
- **Primary category = `Real Estate Agent`** (NOT "Real Estate Agency"). Secondary: `Real Estate Consultant` (≤5 total).
- **Address:** prefer a **visible** brokerage address in/near Downey (with permission) + service areas — Sterling Sky's
  2025-26 testing shows hiding the address can erase the pack. Keep the pin as close to Downey as legitimate
  (proximity ≈ 55% of pack rank). Avoid a shared building crowded with other verified agents.
- Service areas (≤20): Downey, Montebello, Bell Gardens, Pico Rivera, Norwalk, Whittier, Santa Fe Springs,
  Commerce, Bellflower, La Mirada.

- Languages = English + Spanish; attributes: **Identifies as Latino-owned**, Online appointments, Onsite services.
- URL = **jessetek.net** (not jesseonate.com). Hours, DRE #, opening date, **20+ geotagged photos** (build toward 100 — profiles with 100+ photos see ~520% more calls).
- **Track with a geo-grid (Local Falcon/BrightLocal), not GSC average position** — map-pack rank varies block-by-block.

## PRIORITY 1 — REVIEW ENGINE (the #2 factor; currently ZERO)
Reviews feed the pack (~16-20%) AND AI Overviews. **2026: velocity & recency beat raw volume**; rankings decay
if flow stalls ~3 weeks. Sterling Sky's "magic 10" — the biggest jump is crossing **9→10**.
- **1.1 Sprint to 10 real Google reviews in 30-45 days** — personally text the 10-15 most recent closed clients the direct GBP review link.
- **1.2 Install a Jtek (GHL) per-closing system** — "Closed/Funded" trigger → 4-touch SMS+email (Day 0/3/7/30) ≈ 60-70% conversion. Target **≥4 fresh reviews/month forever**. Prompt clients to mention area (Downey/Montebello), buy/sell, and bilingual service — never dictate wording. Bilingual asks for Spanish-dominant clients.
- **1.3 Respond to 100% of reviews <24h, 50+ words, language-matched.** Response rate is now weighted (~+2.3 positions at 75-80%+).
- **1.4 COMPLIANCE (now illegal — FTC ~$51,744/violation):** NO review-gating, NO incentives, NO asking clients to name "Jesse", NO on-premises pressure/kiosks, NO staff quotas. Ask EVERY client the same, offer nothing, let them write freely. Sudden bursts trip Google's spike protection (auto-removal + pause) — steady drip ranks better and is safer.
- **1.5** Seed Zillow & Realtor.com reviews too (corroborates the entity for AI Overviews).

## PRIORITY 3 — CITATIONS & LOCAL LINKS (quality > quantity in 2026)
~20-30 authoritative, locally-relevant citations beat 50 generic; consistent across 15+ platforms ≈ +23% pack likelihood.
- **3.1 Lock ONE canonical NAP against the LIVE GBP** (don't trust old LOCAL-CITATIONS.md address); decide "Onate" (no ñ) once; force every listing → jessetek.net.
- **3.2 Claim the 4 portals** (Zillow, Realtor.com, Homes.com, Redfin) — citation + branded defense + lead capture (links are NOFOLLOW; not a backlink play).
- **3.3 Free high-authority:** Apple Business Connect (Siri/Apple Maps), Bing Places (Copilot), Yelp, Facebook, LinkedIn, Nextdoor.

A DA45 local link beats a DA65 national one. Documented case: NAP cleanup + local links → pack #8→#2-3 in 14 weeks.

- **3.6 DO NOT:** bought links/PBNs/guest-post farms/PR blasts/mass low-DA directory blasts/keyword-stuffed GBP name. 2026 spam updates devalue these (often unrecoverable).

## WHAT CHANGED IN 2025-2026 (don't run 2019 playbooks)
- **AI Overviews** on ~57-83% of queries; cut CTR ~58% but a citation earns ~35-77% more clicks; fed by front-loaded copy (first 30% = 44.2% of citations).
- **Review velocity/recency > volume**; response rate weighted; spike protection auto-pauses bursts.
- **FTC Consumer Review Rule in active enforcement** (~$51,744/violation) — gating/incentives/naming-the-agent/on-premises pressure now banned → the hardcoded 5.0/100 is a real liability.
- **GBP verification defaults to video** for service-area/individual businesses.
- **Doorway/templated city pages suppressed harder**; distrust spreads sitewide.
- **Self-serving review schema = no stars since 2019 + manual-action risk.**
- **HARO/Connectively dead → Featured.com / Qwoted / Source of Sources.**
- **Brand mentions ~3x more correlated with AI visibility than backlinks**; Bing Places + Apple Business Connect feed AI assistants.
- **Instagram public content Google-indexable since 2025-07-10.**

## TIMELINE & KPIs
- **Wk 1-2:** GBP optimized; 5.0/100 schema stripped; canonical NAP locked; first review asks sent.
- **30-60d:** 10+ real reviews; portals + free citations claimed; Downey + 4 core city pages de-duplicated/deep; author blocks live; early geo-grid movement.
- **3-6mo:** ≥4 reviews/mo; 5 local links; Spanish lane live; map-pack movement; GSC impressions climbing.
- **6-12mo:** durable Downey/Montebello pack presence; ~500-1,000 GBP views/mo, 15-30 calls/mo, 100-200 site clicks/mo.
- **Don't quit at month 2** — the most common failure. Local SEO compounds.

## TOP MISTAKES TO AVOID
1. Chasing organic #1 for the bare head term (it's an impressions/authority problem).
2. Treating the 153-page site as the fix while GBP/reviews lag (no GBP+reviews = locked out of the pack).
3. Leaving reviews hardcoded + the fake 5.0/100 schema live (legal + manual-action risk).
4. Keeping 45 doorway clones (suppressed + drags the domain).
5. Reflexively hiding the GBP address; any banned review tactic; keyword-stuffing the GBP name.
6. Buying Yext/mass directory breadth or national links instead of ~20-30 quality citations + a few LOCAL links.
7. Ignoring the Spanish lane — the clearest white-space win.
