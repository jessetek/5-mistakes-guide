## The three winnable surfaces (in priority order)

### 1. The local map pack — HIGHEST impact, Jesse-owned
For "real estate agent in Downey", "realtor near me" while in Downey, Google shows a map +
3 businesses. This is **Google Business Profile**, not the website. Ranking factors:
- **Relevance:** primary category = "Real estate agent"; complete profile; services/products listed.
- **Distance:** proximity of the searcher to the business/service area. Agents should run as a
  **service-area business** (hide street address) covering Downey + neighbors.
- **Prominence:** **review count + velocity + recency**, web citations, and links. Reviews are the
  dominant moveable lever — this is where the next-90-days fight is won.
- The website's job here is supporting: consistent NAP, embedded GBP, schema sameAs to the profile.

### 2. Qualified organic long-tail — MEDIUM impact, shared
These an individual site CAN rank for with a dedicated page + authority:
- "downey real estate agent", "real estate agent in downey ca", "bilingual real estate agent downey",
  "spanish speaking realtor downey", "first time home buyer agent downey", "downey listing agent".
- Each wants its own strong page (or a strong primary page + supporting cluster), internal links,
  E-E-A-T signals (DRE #, reviews, real closings, named author), and FAQ schema.

### 3. Branded — already mostly won, defend it
"jesse onate realtor" ranks ~2.5. Goal: own the whole page-1 (site + GBP + Zillow + Realtor.com +
Yelp) via citations and consistent NAP, so the brand can't be hijacked and warm IG traffic converts.

## Lever priority (impact ÷ effort, this account)
1. **Google Business Profile** (claim/verify/optimize) — *blocks surface #1 entirely.* Jesse.

3. **Citations + NAP consistency** — Tier-1 real estate (Zillow/Realtor.com/Homes.com/Redfin) then

4. **Dedicated "Downey real estate agent" page + internal links** — on-page. **Brain can do now.**
5. **Backlinks** — chambers, local news, partner lenders/escrow, HARO. Jesse (Brain drafts pitches).
6. **Entity/schema polish** — sameAs, aggregateRating, FAQ schema. Brain.

## What NOT to do (penalty risk / waste)
- No bought links or "1000 backlinks" packages.
- No review gating or fake reviews (Google removes them + can suspend the profile).
- No NAP variants — pick ONE format (the LOCAL-CITATIONS.md canonical) and use it everywhere.
- Don't keyword-stuff the GBP business name (suspension risk).
- Don't chase the bare head term organically — it's a directory/map-pack SERP.

## How "rank #1 for real estate agent" actually gets delivered
Not as a single organic #1. As: **map-pack presence in Downey** (GBP) **+** top-10 organic for the
qualified Downey agent terms **+** page-1 domination of the brand. That combination is what puts Jesse
in front of real estate searchers in Downey and what the Brain drives toward.
