## Strategy (one sentence)
The foundation is done — now run a **measurement-driven OFF-PAGE engine** (GBP freshness, review
velocity, local links/PR, citations, AI-search presence) on a fixed cadence, because **~70% of the
remaining win is off-page** and no on-page tweak lifts the impression ceiling (~169 impressions/28d).
Win the **Downey/SE-LA map-pack share** (not a Downey pin), **qualified + Spanish organic**, and **branded**.

  schema, sitemap, internal links, drafts/PRs. Needs the `claude` CLI + git.

## DAILY (cloud-cowork at 9:30pm → a one-tap morning queue)
1. **Reach + integrity health pull (cornerstone).** GBP Performance API (calls, directions, clicks,
   messages, bookings + 2026 surface split: classic / Maps-AI / AI-Overview) + GSC (impressions, clicks,
   position, **distinct-query count**) + indexing/CWV on the 5 money pages. Diff vs. baseline; escalate-on-red.
   Headline = **impression growth + query count**, not position (and during the 2026 GSC ghost-impression
   bug, trust clicks+position, treat raw impressions as suspect). Calls hide in GBP → GBP actions are a first-class conversion.
2. **GBP integrity watch.** Snapshot name/category/address/hours/phone/URL/attributes; alert on ANY
   third-party/Google edit. Guards against a fabricated `aggregateRating` regressing. (revert/appeal = human)
3. **New-review reply (draft).** Detect new Google reviews → AI drafts a ~40–60 word, language-matched
   (EN/ES) reply echoing their neighborhood/transaction; **Jesse one-taps send within 24h**. 1–3★/suspect → Jesse handles.
4. **HARO/journalist sweep.** Featured.com + Source-of-Sources + #journorequest → draft a credentialed,
   non-template bilingual pitch (SoCal/Latino-buyer/NAHREP angle); **Jesse adds a real insight + submits.** ~1–3 links/mo.
5. **Unlinked brand-mention discovery.** Alerts/search for "Jesse Oñate"/"jessetek"/DRE #02133131 → queue
   for the weekly reclamation send. (highest-ROI link play) Plus light sentinels: robots AI-allow check, uptime/canonical, CWV field-data.

## WEEKLY
1. **Review-acquisition push (THE growth lever).** Identical Google review link to every recent client
   ≤48h post-close + 7-day follow-up via the Jtek 4-touch workflow; floor **3–5/mo**. Compliance: ask everyone
   identically — no gating/incentives/dictating wording/naming Jesse; may invite neighborhood/buy-sell/bilingual mention. Never burst. (WHO + warm texts = human)
2. **1 GBP post** (alternate EN/ES) + CTA/UTM to the matching money page (draft → Jesse publishes via Jtek).
   Posts lift panel CTR + ground the Gemini "Ask" answers; they don't move pack rank — freshness/conversion only.
3. **GSC near-miss harvest** (position 8–20 by impressions, EN+ES separately) → ranked win-list + staged on-page edit. ~6× ROI of net-new content; moves in 2–4 weeks.
4. **Unlinked-mention reclamation sends** (3–10/wk): warm article-specific note (draft) → **Jesse sends from his own email**. Prioritize by the linking page's traffic.
5. **Competitor-spam scan + redressal packet** for the Downey/Montebello pack (draft) → **Jesse verifies truly-fake + files**.
6. **Local link-target prospecting:** vetted, relevance-scored list (chambers, NAHREP, BNI, sponsors, partner
   vendors, Latino/Spanish dirs) + drafted outreach. Score by "can an out-of-market competitor replicate this?" (no = moat).
7. **CRO friction review** via Microsoft Clarity on the money pages → staged low-risk fixes. 2026 levers:
   one primary CTA/page, first-person buttons ("Get My Home Value"), multi-step forms opening with an easy question.
8. **Conversion-tracking synthetic check:** submit a test lead through each form; assert GA4 event + Jtek lead landed + server-side dedupe. (broken form = the most expensive bug to miss)
9. **Weekly KPI rollup** → brain + a short digest. Headline = REACH (impressions + query count + SoLV).
   (folded in: full-site crawl for 404/orphan/internal-link health; title/meta CTR test drafts)

## MONTHLY
1. **Geo-grid map-pack scan** (Local Falcon, 5×5/7×7 over Downey+Montebello+SE-LA, EN+ES) → **SoLV**, ARP,
   ATRP + the exact cells you fall out of top-3 = the link/review to-do list. Closes the map blind spot (ledger E2).
2. **Local link & PR execution:** land **1–2 genuinely-local links/mo** (chamber/NAHREP/Board membership,
   sponsorship, or partner preferred-vendor page). Pure Jesse (AI supplies targets+drafts). NAHREP = Spanish-lane moat.
3. **Citation/NAP audit** across top ~15 + vertical + Spanish dirs vs ONE canonical NAP (DRE/Montebello/bilingual).
   Bing Places mandatory (87% of ChatGPT local citations mirror Bing's top-10). (audit auto; logins = human)
4. **AI-search visibility audit:** run core EN+ES prompts through AI Overviews/ChatGPT/Gemini/Perplexity → % cited
   + competitor share. AI packs name only 1–2 businesses + strip the call button — being one of them is the new frontier.
5. **Attribution reconciliation:** GBP actions + GA4 + Instagram → one honest lead total + cost/quality per channel.
6. **Content engine:** advance ONE pillar cluster + a content-decay freshness sweep (answer-first + FAQ schema
   + a real Jesse anecdote each). **GUARDRAIL: no more thin city pages — hold ≤10–15 total; consolidate/enrich/prune
   the ~97%-identical clones (ledger C2). Each survivor ≥60% unique / ≥40% non-scrapeable first-party content.**
7. **Spanish-lane build-out** on `/es/` (self-canonical + symmetric hreflang) targeting `comprar casa con ITIN`,
   `programas para compradores primerizos en California`, `agente de bienes raíces que habla español en Montebello`.
   Native, not machine-translated (Jesse's native pass = the moat).
8. **Maintain Products/Services + answer-first FAQ** (the dead-Q&A replacement that feeds the Gemini "Ask" button:
   Buyer Rep, Listing, First-Time, **Bilingual (Español)**, Relocation, Home Valuation, Investment).
9. **Monthly executive scorecard** (one screen, green/yellow/red + top-3 actions) → brain run-log + ledger.

## KPIs
**LEADING:** GBP posts/wk (≥1, EN/ES) · review-requests ≤48h of close (100%) · new reviews/mo + velocity (floor
3–5; <30d = max weight; baseline 5.0/~20) · local links + earned mentions/mo · mentions reclaimed/mo · citations
fixed + NAP/DRE consistency · **GSC impressions (headline; baseline 169/28d) + growth** · **distinct query count** · near-miss harvested (EN+ES).
**LAGGING:** **SoLV** trend + ARP/ATRP + cells lost · GBP calls/directions/clicks/messages/bookings · GBP impressions
by surface · **organic clicks + avg position (bug-proof North Stars)** · money-page conversion rate · AI-search % cited (EN+ES) · leads by source + branded vs non-branded.
**GUARDRAIL:** zero unreviewed third-party GBP edits · ~100% review-response <24h · zero fabricated aggregateRating · velocity never a burst.
