# Local Citations Playbook — Off-Page SEO

After all the on-page work we've shipped, the highest-impact next step is **local citations** — listings on third-party sites that confirm your business exists with consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone). For local realtor SEO, citations are the second-biggest ranking lever after Google Business Profile.

**Total time:** ~4-6 hours spread over 1-2 weeks. Most listings are free.

---

## The exact NAP info to use everywhere

To avoid Google penalties for inconsistent business signals, **always use this exact wording**:

```
Business name:    Jesse Onate
Business address: 830 N Wilcox Ave, Montebello, CA 90640
Business phone:   (562) 609-4200
Website:          https://jessetek.net/
Email:            jesse@jesseonate.com
Category:         Real estate agent
DRE License:      02133131
Bilingual:        English / Spanish
Service areas:    Downey, Whittier, Pico Rivera, Long Beach, Cerritos,
                  Norwalk, La Mirada, Bellflower, La Habra, Lakewood,
                  Montebello
Hours:            Mon-Sun 8am-9pm PT
Description:      Use the same one as Google Business Profile (the
                  keyword-rich version mentioning bilingual,
                  100+ closings, 5.0 Google rating, DRE #02133131)
```

**Critical:** Don't deviate. "Jesse Oñate" with the ñ vs "Jesse Onate" without — pick one and use it everywhere. Same for "St" vs "Street", "(562)" vs "562-", etc. Google compares these signals literally.

I recommend **without the ñ** (Onate) for citations since GBP is set that way and most directories don't handle special characters cleanly.

## Tier 1 — Real-estate-specific platforms (do these first)

These are the highest-authority citations for realtors. Each takes 5-15 minutes.

### 1. Realtor.com Agent Profile
- URL: https://www.realtor.com/realestateagents/find/
- Search for your name → "Claim your profile"
- **Critical:** make sure the website link points to **jessetek.net**, not jesseonate.com
- Add your photo, bio (use GBP description), service areas
- Add your reviews from Google (Realtor.com pulls them automatically if your DRE is connected)

### 2. Zillow Premier Agent
- URL: https://www.zillow.com/agent-resources/agent-tools/
- The Zillow profile already exists for your business (saw it in search results)
- Confirm: NAP correct, **website = jessetek.net**, photo current
- This is an authority site — your Zillow profile actively backlinks to your site

### 3. Trulia Agent Profile
- URL: https://www.trulia.com/profile/
- Trulia is owned by Zillow, so they often sync. Worth confirming separately anyway.

### 4. Homes.com Agent Profile
- URL: https://www.homes.com/real-estate-agents/
- Free claim. NAP + website + photo + bio.

### 5. RedfinAgent Profile
- URL: https://www.redfin.com/realestate-agents/redfin-partner-agents
- If you've ever closed a deal with a Redfin buyer, you have a profile. Claim it.

### 6. CAR (California Association of Realtors)
- URL: https://www.car.org/membership
- If you're a CAR member, your public profile should link to jessetek.net
- Update via the "Find a Realtor" directory

### 7. National Association of Realtors (NAR)
- URL: https://www.nar.realtor/
- Member directory — confirm website link

## Tier 2 — General business directories (high authority)

### 8. Yelp Business Profile
- URL: https://biz.yelp.com/
- Free claim. Add photos, hours, services.
- **Bonus:** Yelp reviews syndicate to Apple Maps/Siri

### 9. Better Business Bureau (BBB)
- URL: https://www.bbb.org/get-listed
- Free basic listing. Optional accreditation costs ~$500/yr — skip unless you want the badge.

### 10. Yellowpages.com
- URL: https://accounts.yellowpages.com/register
- Free. Old-school but still indexed by Google.

### 11. Apple Business Connect
- URL: https://businessconnect.apple.com/
- Lets you appear in Apple Maps, Siri, Spotlight. **Highly underused** by realtors.
- Verify with your Apple ID. Free.

### 12. Facebook Business Page
- URL: https://www.facebook.com/business/
- If you don't already have one. Even if you don't post, it's a citation.
- Make sure About section has the website link.

## Tier 3 — Local + niche directories

### 13. Downey Chamber of Commerce
- URL: https://www.downeychamber.com/membership/
- Membership ~$200-400/yr. Gets you a local backlink + community visibility.
- Note: Montebello/Downey-area chambers are especially valuable since they match your physical and service areas.

### 14. Whittier Area Chamber of Commerce
- URL: https://www.whittierchamber.com/
- Same play — local backlink + relevance signal for Whittier searches.

### 15. Greater Montebello Chamber of Commerce
- URL: https://www.montebellochamber.org/ (search if URL changes)
- Closest to your registered address — high relevance.

### 16. Local newspapers (free directories)
- Downey Patriot — https://thedowneypatriot.com/
- Whittier Daily News — usually has a business directory

### 17. Nextdoor for Business
- URL: https://business.nextdoor.com/
- Hyperlocal — your actual neighbors see you. Good for word-of-mouth.

### 18. LinkedIn Company Page
- URL: https://www.linkedin.com/company/
- Even if you only have a personal profile, create a company page. Free citation, surfaces in B2B searches.

## Tier 4 — Citation aggregators (set-and-forget)

These services push your NAP to dozens of smaller directories at once. **One-time setup, lasts forever.**

### 19. Yext (paid, ~$199/yr) — strongest
- Auto-syncs NAP across 50+ directories
- One source of truth — you update Yext, all sites update
- Worth it if you want to "set and forget"

### 20. Moz Local (paid, ~$129/yr)
- Same play, slightly fewer directories than Yext

### 21. BrightLocal (paid, audit + cleanup)
- Mostly for auditing existing citations and finding inconsistencies

### Free alternative: do it manually
The Tier 1-3 lists above cover ~80% of what aggregators push to. If you're patient, manual is free.

## Suggested workflow (4 weeks, ~1 hour/week)

**Week 1 (1 hour) — Real estate**
- Claim Realtor.com, Zillow, Trulia, Homes.com, Redfin profiles
- Confirm all link to jessetek.net (not jesseonate.com)

**Week 2 (1 hour) — High-authority general**
- Yelp, BBB, Apple Business Connect, Facebook Business
- Make sure NAP matches GBP exactly

**Week 3 (1 hour) — Local chambers**
- Downey, Whittier, Montebello chambers
- One Patriot/news directory listing

**Week 4 (1 hour) — Audit + cleanup**
- Search "Jesse Onate Realtor" in Google — note every site that comes up
- Click each — check NAP consistency
- Fix anything pointing to jesseonate.com instead of jessetek.net
- Optional: sign up for Yext if you want auto-management

## What this gets you

- **Stronger local-pack ranking** for "Downey realtor", "Montebello realtor", etc.
- **Backlinks from authority sites** (Realtor.com, Zillow, Yelp are DR 80+ sites)
- **NAP signal consistency** — Google's local algorithm reads many sources and rewards alignment
- **Branded search defense** — when someone searches "Jesse Onate Realtor", we want jessetek.net + GBP + Realtor.com + Zillow + Yelp all on page 1, not random old listings

---

## Red flags to avoid

- ❌ **Don't pay for "1000 backlinks"** — those are spam and hurt rankings
- ❌ **Don't list on .ru / .cn / random directories** you've never heard of
- ❌ **Don't use slightly different NAPs** (e.g., "(562) 609.4200" vs "(562) 609-4200") — pick one format, stick to it
- ❌ **Don't change the GBP address** to match a different citation — keep GBP as the source of truth, sync everything else to it
- ❌ **Don't skip claim/verification** when the site asks — unverified listings have less SEO weight

---

## When you're done

Search `"jessetek.net"` in Google in quotes. Count how many third-party sites link to you. The goal is **20+ in 90 days**. After that, momentum builds on its own — newer sites copy older ones.

Citations work in the background. You won't see ranking changes overnight. But in 60-90 days, you'll see "Jesse Onate Realtor" search appear on page 1 with multiple supporting properties, and the local pack will start including jessetek.net more reliably.
